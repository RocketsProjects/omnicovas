PURPOSE
-------
Provide a structured, machine-usable roadmap registry that translates the
OmniCOVAS authority family into phase-by-phase development reality.

This document is the AI companion to the human-readable Development Roadmap
v1.0. It is designed to help future ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and local Soldier
sessions determine:
  - which phase owns a feature;
  - which UI route must contain it;
  - which backend service family must support it;
  - which source/provider constraints apply;
  - what must be proven before phase playbooks are written;
  - what must not be recreated from completed phases;
  - what belongs in roadmap sequencing versus blueprint authority;
  - what phase gates must be satisfied before a phase becomes baseline.
