# OmniCOVAS — Compliance Matrix
### Active Compliance Companion for the OmniCOVAS v5.0 Documentation Family

*Active compliance audit supporting Master Blueprint v5.0 and the current UI, Backend, Source Capability, Roadmap, and Phase Guide authority family. This v4.1 matrix remains active until replaced; stale v4.x authority references are superseded by the v5.0 documentation family.*

**Current authority posture:**
- Supports Master Blueprint v5.0 as the constitutional authority.
- Preserves legal, privacy, ToS, license, attribution, rate-limit, and audit obligations.
- Aligns with native-first voice direction.
- EDDI is cut from current scope and must not be required.
- VoiceAttack is optional adapter only and must not be required.
- OmniCOVAS is the brain.
- No unattended automation, botting, or direct AI in-game action.
- Commander-Confirmed Input Assist remains future/compliance-gated only.

**Prohibited under Law 9 Interpretation B:**

- ❌ Injecting into game process
- ❌ Auto-aim, auto-pilot, auto-trade
- ❌ Bot play or AFK farming
- ❌ Account sharing facilitation
- ❌ Anti-cheat circumvention
- ❌ Redistributing game assets

**Permitted (All Phase 1 Operational):**
- ✅ Reading journal files (documented for tools)
- ✅ Reading Status.json (documented live state)
- ⏳ Reading ModulesInfo.json, Cargo.json, Backpack.json, ShipLocker.json (Phase 2)
- ⏳ Using CAPI per Frontier's terms (Phase 3+, with approval)

**Compliance Status:**
- ✅ All "actions" are advisory (Law 1 Confirmation Gate — operational Phase 1)
- ✅ No automation touches game input
- ✅ Each user has own Frontier account
- ✅ Game files read-only

**Requirements:**

- ✅ No abuse or scraping (architecture committed)

**Architecture Compliance:**
- ✅ OAuth2 PKCE designed
- ✅ Rate limits in Registry (operational)

### 2.3 Trademarks

**Will Do:**
