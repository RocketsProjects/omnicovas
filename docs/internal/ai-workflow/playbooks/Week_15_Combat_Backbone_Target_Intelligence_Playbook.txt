Add combat event constants: TARGET_CHANGED, TARGET_LOST, TARGET_HOSTILE, INTERDICTION_STARTED, INTERDICTION_ESCAPED, INTERDICTION_SUBMITTED, INTERDICTION_FAILED, PVP_CONTACT_DETECTED, PVP_THREAT_FLAGGED, BOUNTY_AWARDED, COMBAT_BOND_AWARDED, COMBAT_RANK_CHANGED, MUNITIONS_CHANGED, COMBAT_ZONE_ENTERED, COMBAT_ZONE_LEFT, COMBAT_THREAT_CHANGED, AX_CONTEXT_DETECTED, ON_FOOT_COMBAT_STATE_CHANGED.

11. Mount the combat router in the attached bridge/router mount file.
12. Add one Target Intelligence UI card. It consumes TARGET_CHANGED/TARGET_LOST over WebSocket and GET /combat/target as fallback. Unknown fields render honestly.
