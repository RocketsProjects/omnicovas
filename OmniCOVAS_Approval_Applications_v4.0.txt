The project is governed by a constitutional framework of Ten Laws and Ten Architectural Principles, with legal compliance as Law 2 (second only to the Confirmation Gate ensuring no AI-driven game actions).

**Our Approach to Your Infrastructure:**

Law 2 (Legal Compliance Protocol) makes every EULA, ToS, and rate limit an absolute constraint. Law 3 (API Respect Protocol) mandates that rate limits are never treated as guidelines. Our architecture specifically implements:
- OAuth2 PKCE for all CAPI authentication
- Rate limiting at ~1 query/minute (well under your 2/sec hard limit)

- No scraping of other commanders' data

- No automation that affects gameplay (Law 1 Confirmation Gate)

The project is transparent by design. Source code, architecture decisions, and compliance posture are publicly auditable at our GitHub repository. I believe this transparency is the right foundation for a tool that interacts with your infrastructure.

I would be grateful for the opportunity to serve the Elite Dangerous commander community with your authorization.

Hello Artie,

o7 Commander,

I'm writing to request whitelisting for OmniCOVAS, an open-source (AGPL-3.0) intelligence suite I've been working on. I wanted to reach out before going live so you know who we are and how we plan to use the Inara API.

**About OmniCOVAS:**
OmniCOVAS is a desktop application (Tauri + Python) that consolidates multiple ED tools into a single unified dashboard with voice integration. Source is at [GitHub URL].

**How We Use Inara:**
We treat Inara as a specialist data source for what only Inara does well — CMDR profiles, faction metadata, community goals, Powerplay information. For market data and live intelligence, we primarily consume EDDN directly and use Spansh bulk dumps. This significantly reduces our live Inara query load.

**Our Rate Limit Discipline:**
- Hard enforcement at 2 requests per minute (never exceeded)
- Event batching where your API supports it (session start/end, docked, FSD jump)
- Aggressive caching: CMDR 24h, factions 6h, CGs 30min, engineers 7d
- Squadron cache pooling — when multiple commanders run OmniCOVAS in a squadron, a single lookup populates all their local caches
- Priority queue for commander-requested vs background lookups
- Graceful degradation at 70% budget used

**What We Contribute:**
We plan to submit commander data (our own users' activity) to enrich Inara — session events, jumps, scans. Our philosophy: contribute more than we query.

**What We'd Like From You:**
- Whitelisting app identifier: `OmniCOVAS`
- Any additional guidelines you'd like us to follow
- Any concerns about our approach

The app will not go live until Phase 3 completion. I'm reaching out now to align with your expectations.

Thank you for everything Inara does for the ED community.

Fly safely,
[Project Creator Name]
OmniCOVAS Project
